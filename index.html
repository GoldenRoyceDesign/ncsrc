The National Cyber Security Research Council (NCSRC) committed to creating a safer digital environment through research, innovation, and practical solutions. With a mission to "Innovate for a Secure Future" NCSRC focuses on strengthening cybersecurityawareness, education, and protection across different sectors. Over the years, it has built a strong community of 100,000+ members and 5,000-professionals, covering 50 districts and successfully addressing 500+ cybersecurity cases.

Its intatives include cyber defense programs, digital forensics training, policy development, and skill-building efforts that help individuals and organizations stay ahead of cyber threats. NCSRC collaborates with government agencies, private sector companies, and academic institutions to develop effective cybersecurity strategies, share knowledge, and create policies that contribute to a more secure digital future.
